# Brand Voice & Copy Standards
> Read this when: writing any user-facing text, marketing copy, UI labels, error messages, or emails.

## Voice

EntrSphere speaks like a **sharp, experienced tech consultant** who's seen projects fail and knows exactly why. Not corporate. Not startup-bro. Confident, direct, slightly provocative.

### Tone Spectrum
- **Homepage/Marketing**: Bold, slightly confrontational ("42% of AI projects failed. Yours won't.")
- **Product UI**: Clear, helpful, professional ("Let's figure out what you actually need")
- **Blog/Content**: Authoritative, educational, opinionated
- **Error states**: Human, honest ("Something broke on our end. We're on it.")

### Do
- Lead with the pain point, then the solution
- Use SA-relevant examples and ZAR pricing
- Reference real stats (42% failure rate, 80% fail at scope)
- Be specific about outcomes ("Crystal-clear spec in 30 minutes")
- Use "we" for EntrSphere, "you" for the customer

### Don't
- Generic startup jargon ("leverage", "synergy", "ecosystem")
- Overpromise ("guaranteed success")
- Condescend to non-technical users
- Use emojis in professional copy (unless social media)
