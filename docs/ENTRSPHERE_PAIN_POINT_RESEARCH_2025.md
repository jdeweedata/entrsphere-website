## Part 2: The Scope Creep Epidemic

### Prevalence Statistics

| Statistic | Source | Impact |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| **52% of all projects experience scope creep** | PMI | "More than half" |
| **78% of projects experience scope creep leading to failure** | Industry Research | "3 in 4 fail due to creep" |
| **Delaying product launch by 6 months = 33% loss of potential earnings** | Industry Research | "R1 in every R3 gone" |
| **68% of developers cite constantly changing requirements as greatest workplace stress** | TechRepublic | "Developer burnout epidemic" |
| **Cost of replacing burned-out developer: 150% of annual salary** | SHRM | "1.5× salary to replace" |
| **33% of all online B2B orders contained errors** in 2025 | Sana Commerce | "One-third wrong" |

### Why Scope Creep Happens

1. **Poorly defined scope at outset** — EntrSphere's core problem to solve
2. **Vague requirements** ("make it intuitive")
3. **Lack of prioritization** (everything is "must-have")
4. **Late stakeholder input** (decision-makers enter late)
5. **Fear of saying no** to leadership/investors
6. **No change management process**
7. **Insufficient user/market research**

**Killer Quote:** "The prompt is not the product. The workflow is the product."
